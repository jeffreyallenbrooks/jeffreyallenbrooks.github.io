I am a 4th year PhD student in the Department of Psychology at New York University, where I work with Jon Freeman in the Social Cognitive and Neural Sciences Lab. My research uses methods spanning social psycholgy, cognitive neuroscience, and computational neuroscience to investigate mechanisms of social and cognitive influences on face perception.

Read more about my research here.
