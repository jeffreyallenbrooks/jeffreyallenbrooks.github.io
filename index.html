Jeffrey A. Brooks

New York University

CV                       Github            Email
I am a 6th year PhD student in the Department of Psychology at New York University, where I work with Jon Freeman in the Social Cognitive and Neural Sciences Lab. My research uses approaches from experimental psycholgy, cognitive neuroscience, and computational neuroscience to investigate mechanisms of social and cognitive influences on face perception.

Read more about my research here.
